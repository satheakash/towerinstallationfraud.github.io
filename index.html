Tower Installation Fraud

Awareness about tower installation fraud

There are lot of peoples who want to install a tower on their land. For that many people search on google. Google shows us many websites but 99% of them are fraud. I also contacted Hindustan Towers Group (Note : Sometimes they will change company name and website name.) for tower installation. This is a big group of fraud people which is active from last 5 years. When I contacted them they convinced me that they are genuine and first took Rs.2250 from me. Later they told me that your land should be commercialized to install the tower for that they took 24000 thousand from me. I didn't believe them at first time but they showed me some fake documents. Later they told me that you will get total 4000000 lacs. They said 400000 lacs you will get in two installments first 1500000 lacs and then remaining 2500000 lacs.They sent me a fake bank message showing that the first installment of 150000 lacs has been stopped for some reason and finally they took 650000 thousand from me. After that I realized that it was a big fraud and in the last 5 years there are many such people with them such fraud was happened. He has cheated more than 10000 people like this. Then I tried to investigate them, I realized that these people are not working from one place, some people are from Uttar Pradesh and some from Haryana. Then I made this website so that this does not happen with anyone. I just want to tell the people that don't contact this peoples. This is a big fraud.
